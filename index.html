Why applying to Founders and Coders

I’ve been studying JavaScript for a while now. I am usually quite good at self-study, I have the grit and concentration. However I feel that right now I would benefit from learning with and from others.

I’ve mainly worked as a Wordpress developer, which has been fine. But in terms of job opportunities I find it limiting. There’s so many exciting jobs and career paths right now for software engineers, but to make the jump it's necessary to have solid JavaScript knowledge.

For instance, I’ve recently applied for a job with a UK Gas provider as creative coder, which is a position that bridges the gap between UX and Programming. That’s a career path that seems very appealing to me. But really my dream job would be in Gov.uk. Mainly because what they do MATTERS and is aimed for EVERYONE. I’ve found interesting that they don’t use any frameworks or libraries, but just JavaScript, HTML and CSS.

I have an appetite and sense of urgency ( because of my age, and why not personality ) that I am sure will make me a good fit for the programme. I will certainly make the most of it, and happily contribute to the cohort. Coding can get lonely, so I look forward to meeting new colleagues for the future.

Founders and Coders feels the right programme for me because:

  1. It's part-time, so I can continue working. Cannot afford doing it on a three months break.
  2. Has a group of people to learn with / from. The collaborative aspect fo the program is a plus.
  3. Strong pre-application requirements, meaning people who get to the application stage have a real interest in coding and have gained some experience along the process.
  4. JavaScript and modern technologies ARE the program
  5. PAIR PROGRAMMING: collaborative coding, learn by explaining, boost your self-confidence, and more!
  6. Is North London based (even though it’s remote, it would be great to meet some local coders)
